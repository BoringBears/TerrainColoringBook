Welcome to TerrainColoringBook!

Do you like coloring books for grown-up? Do you like maps? If so, feel free to download and print these out and color to your heart's content. These are coloring plates that were automatically generated from digital elevation data using a simple Perl script.

Australia

This first set of downloadble images is from Australia! Each image is about two degrees in latitude, or about 150 miles. The names of the files indicate the corner of the area of land from which they are generated. Click here to see all 145 images. Enjoy!

South America

This is South America. Each image is about two degrees in latitude, about 150 miles. The names of the files indicate the corner of the area of land from which they are generated. Click here to see all 332 images.

Africa

Africa is big. And varied. Here are coloring book pages drawn from the vast majority of the land in Africa. Each image is about two degrees in latitude, about 150 miles. The names of the files indicate the corner of the area of land from which they are generated. Here are some samples from the 659 images covering the continent.
