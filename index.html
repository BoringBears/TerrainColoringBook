Welcome to TerrainColoringBook!

Do you like coloring books for grown-up? Do you like maps? If so, feel free to download and print these out and color to your heart's content. These are coloring plates that were automatically generated from digital elevation data using a simple Perl script.

Australia

This first set of downloadble images is from Australia! Each image is three degrees in latitude, or about 210 miles. The names of the files indicate the corner of the area of land from which they are generated. Click here to see all 145 images. Enjoy!

This is South America. Each image is three degrees in latitude, about 210 miles. The names of the files indicate the corner of the area of land from which they are generated. Images coming soon...
